1. Director Overview & Roles

The Hospital Director module serves executive leadership and their support staff. Access is permission-driven — no widget or report appears unless explicitly granted in Access Control.

👔
Hospital Director / CEO Full executive suite — all dashboards, management reports, and strategic scorecards at /portal/hub/director.
🩺
Medical Director Clinical quality focus — daily census, ER flow, clinical alerts, morbidity/mortality, infection control reports.
📊
Finance Director / CFO Financial tab, monthly P&L, unit economics (pharmacy, lab, radiology, theatre, IPD), AR ageing.
🤝
Assistant Director Operational oversight at /portal/hub/assistant-director — beds, flow, revenue, alerts (subset of director suite).
📋
Director's Secretary Executive support at /portal/hub/secretary — calendar, briefings, management reports (read), correspondence.
🔐
System Administrator Configures permissions at /hms-admin/access — grants report sections, KPIs, panels per role.

2. Director Portal Hub

The Director Portal is the landing page for hospital executives. It hosts the React-powered DirectorPortalShell with tabbed access to daily, weekly, monthly, and annual views — each visible only when the user's role has the corresponding permission.

Portal navigation

  1. Sign in with a director-enabled role — home portal routes to /portal/hub/director.
  2. Use the Director reports tab bar to switch between Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Annual views.
  3. URL parameter ?report=weekly deep-links to a specific report (e.g. share with board members).
  4. Sidebar tile Management Reports opens the full live report catalog at /management-reports.

6. Annual Performance Scorecard

The Annual Scorecard provides a board-ready view of hospital performance across five strategic domains, with multi-year trend analysis and domain radar visualization.

Clinical quality Financial health Patient experience Workforce Safety & compliance

Scorecard panels

  • Overall performance — hero summary with composite score
  • Domain radar — pentagonal radar chart across five domains
  • 5-year performance — longitudinal trend for strategic planning

Each domain has granular ACL permissions (director.annual.domain.*) so boards can share clinical quality without exposing financial detail, or vice versa.

Use the annual scorecard for board meetings, donor reports, and ministry submissions. Export via Print from Management Reports or portal browser print for PDF archival.

Using management reports

  1. Open /management-reports from sidebar, portal tile, or reports hub.

12. Permissions & Access Control

Director module visibility is entirely permission-driven. Administrators configure access at /hms-admin/access.

Permission layers

LayerExample permissionsEffect
Module entryhms_reports.read, director.dashboard.readCan open management reports or director portal
Full suitehms_reports.fullAll tabs, cards, KPIs, and panels
Section tabhms_reports.dailyEntire Daily tab in management reports
Individual cardhms_reports.daily.censusSingle report card only
Dashboard tabdirector.dashboard.tab.revenueRevenue tab on daily dashboard
KPI widgetdirector.dashboard.kpi.bedsBed occupancy KPI tile
Panel widgetdirector.weekly.panel.doctor_perfDoctor performance panel

Granting access — step by step

  1. Open /hms-admin/access and select the target role (e.g. Hospital Director, Assistant Director).
  2. Under Permissions, enable hms_reports.read or hms_reports.full.
  3. Grant section permissions (hms_reports.daily, etc.) or individual card permissions as needed.
  4. Under UI visibility, enable director dashboard KPIs, panels, and tabs.
  5. Assign the director portal as home portal under Role portals.
  6. Save — user sees changes on next page load.

Role profiles

Deployment bootstrap may auto-grant director permissions to roles matching Hospital Director, CEO, or Medical Director titles. Custom roles require manual ACL configuration.

16. Executive Decision Workflows

Morning operational review

  1. Open Director Portal → Daily dashboard.
  2. Review bed occupancy and ER wait KPIs — escalate if thresholds exceeded.
  3. Check Critical alerts panel — deaths, ICU census, incidents.
  4. Switch to Management Reports Daily tab for narrative detail on census, theatre, pharmacy.
  5. Share key figures in stand-up or secretary briefing pack.

Weekly leadership meeting

  1. Open Weekly Performance Report (portal tab or management reports Weekly tab).
  2. Compare patient volume and occupancy vs. prior week.
  3. Review HR staffing card — address absenteeism and overtime trends.
  4. Discuss quality & safety incidents and supply chain alerts.
  5. Print weekly tab for meeting minutes attachment.

Monthly board pack

  1. Generate Monthly P&L from director portal.
  2. Export Management Reports monthly tab (Print → Full PDF).
  3. Include clinical performance and patient satisfaction cards.
  4. Cross-check with Finance module statements (/financials).
  5. Present annual scorecard if quarter-end or year-end.

Capacity & escalation triggers

19. Troubleshooting
